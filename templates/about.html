Recode Hackloop 2024

This project was done for the Hackloop 2024's Open Innovation theme by

Akansh , Aryan , Keerthan .





M.E.A.L.S (Meal Efficiency & Automated Logistics System) was built to make the daily lives of hostelers (specially NMAMIT)
easy. Basically the hostelers have a primitive coupon system for mess which causes a lot of inconveniences (atleast to Aryan)
so this idea came to existence, the idea of having a digitalized attendance system also with the facility to know the amount of
traffic to the mess owners so that the analysis can be made and most important food wastage can be reduced...
